Instructions

  1. After clicking play in the Home tab .

  2. First you will submit the range you want to guess the random number from. For example 1 to 10, 10 to 100 etc.

  3. A random number will be choosen from the range you have specified.

  4. Then try and guess the random number, by submitting your guesses.

  5. If you fail to guess the random number correctly, hints will be provided for you.

The following are the hints:

  • Your guess was low, please enter a higher number
  • Your guess was high, please enter a lower number
  • The comp_number is a multiple of 2
  • The comp_number is a multiple of 3
  • The comp_number is a multiple of 5

With the help of these hints, I hope you will be able to guess the random number.

When you enter the correct random number, you will receive the following message Yeah you\'ve got it, this is your score

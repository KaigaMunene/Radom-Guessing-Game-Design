Instructions

The computer will choose any random number between 1 and the range you have specified. So you will specify the end of the range maybe 10, 20, 100, 1000 etc.

Then the you will try to guess the right number.

If the user failed to enter the random number chosen by the computer then the user will get a hint.

The hints will be like these:

  • Your guess was low, please enter a higher number.
  • Your guess was high, please enter a lower number
  • The comp_number is a multiple of 2
  • The comp_number is a multiple of 3
  • The comp_number is a multiple of 5

With the help of these hints, you have to find the random number choosen by the computer.

When you will enter the right random number chosen by the computer you will get an output like this:
Yeah you\'ve got it, this is your score
